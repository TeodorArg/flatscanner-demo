# Review Categories

Use these categories consistently across providers and prompts:

- `pests`
- `damage`
- `missing_essentials`
- `mold_damp_smell`
- `temperature`
- `cleanliness`
- `safety`
- `host_conflict`
- `listing_mismatch`
- `noise`
- `checkin_access`
- `window_view`

Guidance:

- Treat `window_view` as important user-facing context. It can be positive or negative.
- Escalate `pests`, `mold_damp_smell`, `safety`, and severe `damage` as high-severity candidates.
- Keep conflict/dispute signals separate from ordinary inconvenience complaints.
